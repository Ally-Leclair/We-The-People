The Marsh

The marsh is a symbol for Kya. It is seen as this place unknown and unexplored by most people. The marsh is full of wildlife and curiosities unknown to society outside of it; similarly, no one really knows anything about Kya, which is why all of the teenagers in the town are intrigued by Kya. The marsh is nothing but a waste of space to the townspeople, and to them, Kya is “marsh trash”.
